product detail

An essential piece to your homebrew kit. The 5 Gallon Carboy is crafted from quality heavy-duty ribbed glass providing you with the strongest glass carboy available. Although heavier and more cumbersome than plastic fermenters many brewers prefer glass to plastic because it's difficult to scratch, creating crevices for bacteria to hide.

*Carboys are heavy and slick, so we recommend caution when moving them. The last thing you want is your precious brew landing on the floor. We recommend the purchase of a handle or Brew Hauler Carboy Carrier .

This carboy uses stopper size #7.

General Info
  • 5 Gallons
  • Heavy Duty Ribbed Glass
  • Use carboy stopper size #7 for 5 gallon glass carboys
